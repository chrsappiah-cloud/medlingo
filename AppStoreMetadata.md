## Version 1.0 Information

### Description
Medlingo is your AI-powered medical terminology tutor. Master complex medical vocabulary through engaging AI avatar sessions, interactive lessons, pronunciation practice, and spaced repetition — designed for medical students, nursing professionals, and healthcare workers.

Key Features:
• AI Avatar Tutor — Learn from an engaging virtual instructor who explains medical terms with synchronized speech and visual aids
• Audio Pronunciation — Hear correct pronunciations with phonetic breakdowns
• Structured Chapters — Progress through anatomy, pathology, pharmacology, and more
• Spaced Repetition — Smart review system that optimizes long-term retention
• Progress Tracking — Monitor your learning journey with detailed analytics
• Offline Support — Download lessons for study anywhere, anytime

Whether you're preparing for medical exams, starting clinical rotations, or building your healthcare vocabulary, Medlingo makes learning medical terminology accessible and engaging.
